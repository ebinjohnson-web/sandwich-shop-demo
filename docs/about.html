About the brand

A local lunch spot with a clearer feel.

A simple brand story for a sandwich shop that wants to feel warm, useful, and easy to trust.

Shop story

Made for people who want a good lunch without the rush.

Brick & Board is built around a simple promise: good bread, satisfying sandwiches, and a lunch break that feels a little easier. The shop tone stays warm, but never fussy.

Fresh prep Daily bread, crisp greens, and quick counter-ready service.
Short menu Enough variety without making ordering slow.
Group options Catering paths that feel easy for offices and events.
Believable demo tone Designed to pitch well to real cafés, delis, and lunch shops.
What the brand stands for

Warm, local, and easy to trust.

These are the small cues that make the concept feel grounded instead of generic.
